Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is this college NAAC accredited?

Yes, the college is NAAC accredited and follows quality education standards.

Is the college affiliated with a recognized university?

Yes, the college is affiliated with a recognized state university.

What courses are offered by the college?

The college offers UG, Junior College, and professional courses such as B.Sc IT, B.Com, BMS, and BA.

Does the college provide placement assistance?

Yes, placement assistance and career guidance are provided to final-year students.

What is the admission process?

Admissions are based on merit and eligibility criteria defined by the university.

Is online admission available?

Yes, students can apply for admission through the online admission portal.

What are the eligibility criteria for UG courses?

Students must have passed 12th from a recognized board in the relevant stream.

Does the college offer scholarships?

Yes, government and merit-based scholarships are available for eligible students.

Is there a library facility?

Yes, the college has a well-equipped library with books, journals, and digital resources.

Are hostel facilities available?

Hostel facilities are available on a limited basis or through nearby accommodations.

Does the college have a computer laboratory?

Yes, modern computer labs with internet access are available for students.

What are the college timings?

College timings vary by course but generally run from morning to afternoon.

Is attendance mandatory?

Yes, students must maintain minimum attendance as per university norms.

Does the college organize cultural events?

Yes, annual cultural festivals, seminars, and events are regularly organized.

Are sports facilities available?

Yes, the college provides indoor and outdoor sports facilities.

Is there a grievance redressal system?

Yes, students can raise issues through the grievance redressal committee.

Does the college support internships?

Yes, internship opportunities and industry exposure are encouraged.

Are exams conducted semester-wise?

Yes, exams are conducted as per semester pattern defined by the university.

Is there a canteen facility?

Yes, hygienic and affordable food is available in the college canteen.

Does the college provide career counseling?

Yes, career guidance sessions are conducted regularly.

Is the campus Wi-Fi enabled?

Yes, Wi-Fi connectivity is available within the campus.

Does the college encourage research activities?

Yes, students are encouraged to participate in research and project work.

Are extracurricular activities compulsory?

Participation is encouraged but not compulsory for all activities.

Does the college have an alumni association?

Yes, the college maintains an active alumni network.

Is there an anti-ragging committee?

Yes, the college strictly follows anti-ragging guidelines.

What documents are required for admission?

10th & 12th marksheets, leaving certificate, ID proof, and photographs.

Can students apply for education loans?

Yes, admission letters are provided for education loan purposes.

Is practical training included in courses?

Yes, practical sessions and lab work are part of the curriculum.

Does the college conduct parent-teacher meetings?

Yes, meetings are conducted to discuss student progress.

How can I contact the college for more information?

You can contact the college via email, phone, or visit the contact page.
