Course Overview

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) is a three-year undergraduate program that provides strong knowledge in accounting, finance, taxation, economics, business law, and management principles. The course prepares students for careers in commerce, finance, banking, and corporate sectors.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 12th (HSC) from Commerce stream
  • Students from Arts / Science stream may apply (as per college rules)
  • Basic knowledge of Accountancy preferred

Skills You Will Learn

  • Financial Accounting & Auditing
  • Business & Corporate Law
  • Taxation (Direct & Indirect)
  • Financial Management
  • Analytical & Decision-Making Skills
