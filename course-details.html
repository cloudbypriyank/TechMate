Course Details

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) is a three-year undergraduate degree offered by University of Mumbai. The course focuses on accounting, finance, taxation, economics, and business law.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidate must have passed HSC (12th) from Commerce or Science stream from a recognized board.

Admission Process

Admission is based on merit as per University of Mumbai guidelines. Students should regularly check the college notice board.
